E-Voting System

About the System

The Regional Maritime University E-Voting System is a secure and transparent platform designed to facilitate university elections. It provides a convenient way for students to participate in the democratic process of electing their representatives.

Key Features

  • Secure authentication with email OTP verification
  • Real-time election results and analytics
  • Transparent voting process
  • Mobile-friendly interface for voting from any device
  • Comprehensive admin dashboard for election management

How It Works

  1. Login with your RMU student email
  2. Verify your identity with your student ID
  3. Receive a one-time password (OTP) via email
  4. Access the voting dashboard
  5. Cast your vote in active elections
  6. Receive a confirmation receipt

Security Measures

Our system employs multiple layers of security to ensure the integrity of the voting process:

  • Two-factor authentication (Email + Student ID)
  • One-time password verification
  • Prevention of duplicate voting
  • Secure database storage
  • Audit trails for all voting activities
